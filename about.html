"Common Loon.", Identification, All About Birds. The Cornell Lab of Ornithology, n.d. Web. 25 Aug. 2016.

The eerie calls of Common Loons1 echo across clear lakes of the northern wilderness2. Summer adults are regally patterned3 in black and white4. In winter, they are plain gray above and white below5, and you’ll find them close to shore on most seacoasts6 and a good many inland reservoirs and lakes7. Common Loons are powerful, agile divers that catch small fish in fast underwater chases8. They are less suited to land9, and typically come ashore only to nest10.
